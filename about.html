About

Hi, I'm Erica and I work at the intersection of technology and creativity to help businesses grow.

I majored in Art History because of my passion for design. I combined my creativity with an aptitude for technology by exploring blogging and started and deleted more websites than I can count! As soon as the site was live I would lose interest in generating content.

It wasn't until recently that I realized my passion for building websites was just that - the act of creation using technology to make an idea come to life! The lightbulb went off that I should get back into coding and pursue Front-end Development, where I could combine technology and structure with the art of creating something new.

I love helping clients strategize on building their brand and business and helping them achieve their vision through Digital Marking and Website Development. It's great being intellectually challenged to solve problems and learn new things every day.

When I'm not busy staring at a screen, you can find me outside enjoying nature and gardening. I like trying to take decent photos, and miss my days of living in NYC, where you're constantly presented with new and interesting life on the street. All photographs on this site except the headshot are my own. I also enjoy lounging on the couch with a good book and a cozy cat nearby.
