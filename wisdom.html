Wisdom Flowing

Role: Website Design
Tools used: Figma, Squarespace

This project involved desigining a website concept for a Qigong instructor whose business failed to launch due to COVID-19 pandemic hitting right when the design was in progress.

After the graphic designer Michael Jewell developed the brand identity, I came up with the content and design for the home page and began to build it out. Content was ready for a couple of additional pages but I did not get to the design work on those since things were put on hold.

The brand concept was to be calm and in sync with nature and a healthy way of life.
