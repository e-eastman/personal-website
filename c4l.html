Coaching 4 Life

Role: Digital Marketing, Social Media Graphics, Branding. Website Design & Dev coming soon!
Tools used: Figma, Canva, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Mailchimp

This is an ongoing freelance client project for a life coach that is working to build a business on the side. We're slowing making progress in building out her brand.

The brand concept is holistic medicine for the spirit and mind and embodies calm, inspiration and fun.

I started the branding process by building a moodboard and getting feedback from the client about colors and overall brand feel.

I used a logo generator tool to develop a concept, and took that output to refine the idea and create the logo in Illustrator.

This client was already using Mailchimp to send out emails, but didn't have cohesive design to reflect her brand, so the emails had a random feel. I designed a basic template for regular newsletters that is used to showcase new content such as videos and audio meditations.

Using this template helps her audience to recognize her brand. I create new emails and ensure that they go out on a regular schedule so her audience knows what to expect and when to expect it, building trust in her brand.

As part of Digital Marketing support, I also design a variety of graphics for social media including YouTube thumbnails and cover art for audio meditations.

The next project is to create YouTube channel art and a Facebook page banner to begin to roll out the new branding, with a full website to follow.
